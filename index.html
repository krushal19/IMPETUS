How To Use IMPETUS Lubricants

Proper use of our high-performance lubricants ensures optimal engine protection and extended life for your vehicles and machinery.

Check Engine/Component
Ensure the vehicle or machinery is turned off and positioned safely before starting the lubrication process.
Apply Lubricant Properly
Use the recommended quantity and apply directly to the required part like engine, bearings, or gears.

Start the engine or machine and observe for smooth performance and reduced friction or noise.
