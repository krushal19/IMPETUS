About IMPETUS Lubricants

IMPETUS Lubricants Industries, established in 2010, is a renowned manufacturer, supplier, and exporter of premium-grade engine oils, greases, and lubricant solutions. Known for our unwavering commitment to quality and innovation, we’ve earned a trusted name among industrial users and vehicle owners across India.

Our product lineup includes high-performance solutions like EP-0 Grease, API GL5 Gear Saver, Synthetic Oils, Rust Cleaners, and advanced CNG oils — all engineered for durability, efficiency, and protection under India’s toughest conditions.

Backed by a growing network of dealers and partners, we continue to serve mechanics, workshops, and automotive enthusiasts who demand performance without compromise.

Our Mission

To offer innovative, high-performance lubricant solutions for every engine type, ensuring peak performance and sustainability.

Our Vision

To become a benchmark Indian lubricant company globally admired for quality, performance, and responsibility.
